This graph shows single-level indexed placement of a filesystem on a graph.
You may choose one of the given file creation/removal array or enter your own.
Each event consists of a filename and it's size (in blocks) or - (minus) if file is to be removed.
Each filename is unique single character. Operations are separated by a semicolon (;).
You may create up to 10 files. Disk is limited to 50 blocks.

A,2;B,3;A,-;C,4;D,5;B,-;E,15
A,4;B,3;C,6;D,5;B,-;E,5;A,-;F,10
A,2;B,3;C,4;D,5;B,-;E,7;D,-;F,10;A,-;G,1;H,1;G,-;I,10;J,8;I,-

% of remaining files are fragmented (consisting of more than 1 fragment).
% of used space are allocated to fragmented files.
